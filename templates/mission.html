Our Mission

To empower every trader with professional-grade algorithmic trading tools through open-source innovation and community collaboration.

Breaking Down Barriers in Algorithmic Trading

Our mission is crystal clear: to eliminate the technological and financial barriers that have traditionally kept sophisticated trading tools in the hands of large institutions. We believe that innovation in financial technology should benefit everyone, not just those with the deepest pockets.

Through our open-source platform, we're creating a level playing field where individual traders can access the same caliber of tools and infrastructure that power the world's largest trading operations.
